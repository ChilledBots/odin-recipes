Description

Ingridients

  • Meat: 1/2 lb ground pork and 1/2 lb lean ground beef
  • Diced Onion
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • A dash of sugar

Steps

  1. Cook the meat until browned and crumbly
  2. Add onion. Cook until onion is translucent
  3. Add tomato and rest of ingridients
  4. Cook the noodles
  5. Make the cheese layer
